Operational Advice

After harvest you got a lot of the logistics out of the way. Here's a quick checklist to make sure you didn't miss anything.

  1. Sign a contract
  2. Source seeds
  3. Buy fertilizer
  4. Buy fuel for next harvest and plant time
  5. If necessary, set up Visas for migrant workers
